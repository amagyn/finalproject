Brand Deals

One of the earliest questions aspiring influencers will ask
themselves is: How do I start working with brands?
An influencer's inbox — whether it's email or direct messages on social media — is
often where that process of getting in contact with a brand begins.
Many influencers, like Gigi Kovach (a part-time lifestyle influencer),
go straight to email to reach out to brands for collaborations. What
do you say when reaching out to brands? Kovach told Insider that
she keeps a "pitch bank" of email templates handy when she's approaching brands
and potential sponsors.



Meanwhile, other influencers will contact brands
by cold DMing them on social media, like Instagram for a collaboration.

Micro influencer Ashley Jones said that her
strategy consists of posting on Instagram about products she loves and tagging the brand.
She then reaches out to the brand to introduce herself and
explains why she'd like to work with them.
She'll often ask for a proper PR contact too, she said.





LinkedIn is also a tool creators
utilize to connect with PR reps or influencer-marketing
contacts at brands. For instance, TikTok star London Lazerson uses LinkedIn to DM CEOs.
