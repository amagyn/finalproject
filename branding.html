Branding

Branding is one of, if not the most critical elements, when it
comes to becoming a succesful content creator.
Connecting with an audience, building a community of people who care about
the type of content you release, and ultimately establishing
a deeper connection with your audience is vital.
To build a strong brand as a content creator, you
want the personality of your content to be crystal clear.
In other words, be consistent with who you are and how you portray yourself through your content.
Is your content fun, trendy, and bold? If so, you don’t want to contradict those brand qualities by randomly
showing up, as serious, bland, and reserved.
Or, vice-versa. Your brand’s personality traits should be consistently
infused in all of your content.
This includes your environment,
content features, the colors and style of your content,
the words you use, and if you’re on camera, your delivery.

STEPS TO BRANDING

      1. Identify your Audience
      2. Develop your Brand Position
      3. Pick a Brand/Budiness name
      4. Establish your brand's look/aesthetic
      5. Create a logo
      6. Integrate your brand everywhere
